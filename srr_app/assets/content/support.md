# Support

Need help with Carrom SRR?

- Email: support@example.com
- Response window: 1-2 business days
- Include tournament name, device type, and screenshots when reporting issues

## Common Support Topics

- Login and account access
- Tournament setup and workflow
- Player upload and ranking upload
- Match, round, and standings display
- Web and mobile sync issues

## Escalation

If your issue is tournament-blocking, mention **URGENT** in the email subject.
