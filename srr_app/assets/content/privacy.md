# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: February 19, 2026_

Carrom SRR is operated by The Khatu Family Trust.

## What We Collect

- Account data: name, email, role
- Tournament data: players, rankings, rounds, scores, standings
- Operational data: feedback submissions and diagnostic logs

## How We Use Data

- Authenticate users and secure access
- Run tournament workflows and standings
- Provide support and improve product quality

## Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data is shared only with trusted service providers required to operate the app (for example Firebase services).

## Data Retention

Data is retained as needed for tournament operations, history, auditing, and support. You can request deletion where legally and operationally permitted.

## Security

We apply technical and administrative controls to protect account and tournament data. No system can be guaranteed 100% secure.

## Your Choices

- Request profile updates
- Request access or deletion (where applicable)
- Contact support for privacy-related concerns
